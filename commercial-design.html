ABOUT THE PROJECT


The concept is a mobile application game for kids from 5 years and up. We wanted to work with music and games. In Opera Dreams you play the role of Farinelli, a wannabe opera star. Farinelli is on the journey from amature singer to opera diva. Farinelli will face challenges along the way and you will have use Farinelli’s “amazing” voice to shutter glass, sing for helpful birds to get through obstacles and much more. Farinelli can also use stomach powers to escape from dogs by “turbo-farting” or fart the way through crowds of people. The main feature of the game is to use the microphone in a whole new way. Use the microphone in your mobile phone to imitate the bird song, to outperform the street musician, to simply make a farting sound and scream to scare the phantom of the Opera. Other ways to interact is to imitate the pattern you physically move your phone in the shape of the pattern.

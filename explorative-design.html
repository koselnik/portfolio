ABOUT THE PROJECT

The Super Power Swings Gosl채skig/Cozy-scary The aim of this design is to allow fantasy to flow, give a scary feeling and at the same time let kids relax. We wanted to create a place where the children don't have to play chaotic and wild games. The Gosl채skig is intended to be for relaxation and coziness. It is inspired by a cave and has predefined sounds, but also leaves the children to create their own world inside. We started out with some keywords like: "Cozy", "Massage", "Breathing room", and "Secret room". DISCO FOREST The aim of this concept was designed to allow the children to create music in nature with nature. The idea is for each tree to have an instrument as its personality, the drums, the base, the voice and the synthesizer. The speakers are hidden on top of the trees, creating a feeling that the forest is their discotheque and giving them a place to dance. The instruments and speakers are built in the trees. A magical place where you can explore and create music together. We found inspiration in a project that uses plants as musical instruments and we found the perfect place to place it at V책rby G책rd during our field trip.

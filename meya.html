MEYA

Partners: Moa Bergsmark, Anna Movin and Evert Lagerberg

Scope: 6 weeks, halftime

ABOUT THE PROJECT

Meya is an interactive bag, it gives you feedback when you are on the move and it's hard to hear your smartphone ringing. In this project we made a physical prototype of a bag. The face of the bag discretely lights up and starts moving when a connected smartphone gets a call. If you want to, you can reject the call with the "do-not-disturb" ball. The bag can also help you to automatically mute the phone when you open the handle. This was a very fun project. We have participated in a student challenge in Stanford, California and we are participating in an academic conference in Danmark as well.

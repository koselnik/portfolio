Scope: 4 weeks

ABOUT THE PROJECT

MindBox is a concept develop for Interaction Design 1 course. The concept is a product created to help elderly people with memory impairment. The concept includes a box and a wrist gadget with an appealing retro design for the elderly. The main purpose is to remind the users of their daily activities and appointments, and thereby empower them in their daily life. MindBox can record and listen to messages the user, relatives or caregivers creates. The comments about the MindBox from our target group were positive, they think technology today is hard to understand and use. Link to the video: https://vimeo.com/59738429

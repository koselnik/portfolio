Responsible re-design

Role: Concept developer, prototype

Partners: Mona Salmani

Scope: 4 weeks, halftime

ABOUT THE PROJECT

The brief for this design was to design for a target group often forgotten or in need of attention, we were to take a design already existing and redo it. Mona and I worked with the website “Integration in Sweden”, www.integrationinsweden.se. It was created as project to guide and assist people who are new in Sweden. We came up with a re-design for their website and service, which we called NySvenskar. It is an improvement of “Integration in Sweden”, its design focuses on finding vocational mentoring. We worked in an iterative process, in a 4-week period halftime, with critique sessions every week where we got some feedback from our teachers and classmates. We used an online mock-up tool called MockFlow to make the first digital mock up. We later used a service called “Wix” in which we created the final prototype.

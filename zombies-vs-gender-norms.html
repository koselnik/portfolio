Zombies vs Gender norms

Role: Concept, idea, analysis, user tests, illustration

Partner: Lydia Wallgren

Scope: 8 weeks, halftime

ABOUT THE PROJECT

This project was done as an individual course in HCI at KTH in the spring of 2014. The starting point was to investigate how a board can be used to facilitate discussion of the concept of gender and norms around gender . The work began with an analysis of how gender is portrayed in popular culture with a focus on games and especially board games. It was clear that female characters were underrepresented in the game , both men and women were portrayed in a prescriptive way and that female characters sexualiserades to a greater extent than male characters . We also noticed that many games , like popular culture in general , lacked a diversity of underrepresented groups.
